Policy and Ethics for Experiments

Course note of A/B testing on Udacity: Lesson 2 Policy and Ethics for Experiments Main principles 1. Risk what risk is the participant undertaking? The main threshold is whether the risk exceeds that of “minimal risk”. Minimal risk is defined as the probability and magnitude of harm that a participant would encounter in normal daily life. The harm considered encompasses physical, psychological and emotional, social, and economic concerns. If the risk exceeds minimal risk, then informed consent is required.
